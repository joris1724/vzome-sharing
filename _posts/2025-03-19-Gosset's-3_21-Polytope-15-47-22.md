<a href="https://vorth.github.io/vzome-sharing/2005/09/18/gossets-polytopes.html?id=gossets-polytopes">Scott</a> made a vZome model of the Gosset's 3_21 polytope<a href="https://joris1724.github.io/vzome-sharing/2025/03/19/16-39-47-Gosset's-3_21-Polytope/">.</a> I could not neglect his remark <i>Constructing this in real Zome would be a real challenge!</i>. Therefore a perl script cleaned up his model a bit by eliminating all (partially) overlapping struts. Also (only) one set of crossing struts had to be replaced (<i>manually</i>) by intersecting struts. The resulting vZome model makes it easy to build a Zometool implementation.

Unfortunately, for the actual physical build, I had to few hb1 struts. Only two options are available for a compromise. I used both in the following model. In between the teal and pink connectors, I simply dropped the required hb1 struts, and for the blue struts emanating from the pink connectors I used a b2 bending over a b1, instead of 2hb2's connected to 2hb1's.

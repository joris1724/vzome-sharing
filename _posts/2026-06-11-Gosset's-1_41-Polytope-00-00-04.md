This model shows orthographic projections of both the 7-demicube (Gosset's 1_41) and the 10-demicube (Gosset's 1_71). These have only <b>64 distinct 3D ball positions</b> after projection. In the Zometool model, these will be represented by <b>brown/orange/red</b> connectors. Due to the projection from 7D or 10D to 3D, 592 pairs of crossing struts must be dealt with. 292 of these intersections have been replaced by <b>false vertices</b>, each connecting at least four Zometool struts. These false vertices are represented by <b>white</b> connectors:

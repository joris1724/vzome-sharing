The 4D analog of the great stellated dodecahedron. As all star polytopes, it has full hexacosichoric symmetry. Its dual is the grand hexacosichoron.
<p>
A <a href="https://polytopologist.github.io/zome_pages/finalstellation120cell.htm">Zometool model</a> was constructed for the first time in 2007.
<p>
In this vZome model, each orange ball connects y00, y0, b0 and b1 struts. As y00 struts were never mass produced, one can replace these by bending a b2 over a y1 strut. In this particular configuration, it does not put too much strain on the struts and neighbouring connectors. It does not alter the proposed sequence of steps: in step 32 a y1 strut has to be introduced (instead of an orange ball and y00, y0 and b1 struts), and in step 51 a b2 has to be put in, bending over the y1 strut (instead of connecting a b1 to the orange ball).

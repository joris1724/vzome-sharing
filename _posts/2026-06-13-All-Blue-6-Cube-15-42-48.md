<a href="https://www.facebook.com/mananself/posts/pfbid06RsV4Q9TLey1wDrgdpiq2Pxohdc5CpEoUmrCe1pPMugBPrziS9F8guDZmiTy8vU1l">Nan Ma</a> made five vZome 3D projections of the 6-cube which could be built with Zometool struts of the same color. Apparently, these struts also have the same length. Three of the models do not suffer from crossing struts due to the projection from 6D to 3D. Paulo Freire made physical models for these three.
<p>
Unfortunately, for the <i>all-blue</i> projection, 60 pairs of crossing struts must be dealt with in the physical Zometool model. 54 of these intersections have been replaced by <b>false vertices</b>, each connecting four regular Zometool struts. These false vertices are represented by <b>blue</b> connectors. The 6-cube only has <b>81 distinct 3D ball positions</b> after projection. These are represented by <b>white</b> connectors.
